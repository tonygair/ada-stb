# Thanks

This work owes a debt to the Ada community who have answered
questions, corrected mistakes, and pointed at the right manual
page over decades — on `comp.lang.ada`, on the
[Ada Forum](https://forum.ada-lang.io), on the AdaCore and Alire
issue trackers, on Reddit's `r/ada`, and in every other quiet
corner where a beginner asks something obvious and someone
takes the time to answer well.

The Dark Factory's bindings line is for them, and for everyone
still picking the language up. If something here is wrong,
it's our mistake; if something here is right, it's because
people taught us.

This dedication is in lieu of names. Most of the people who
helped us along the way will never see this file, and naming
some would slight the rest. The community as a whole is the
right object of thanks.
